Free bird

Synonymous with Andy Warhol. Repetition art explores the contrasting similarities shared by advertising and the re-occurring archetypes in popularized media. My personal incentives share no similarities to this ethos. The original hand drawn pencil sketch now resides with a friend. My only copies up until now have all been digital. Thankfully, the fab lab has afforded me an opportunity to own multiple unique renditions of this piece of art. I am very great full.

Ilen engraving

During the Ilen educational pack project. I found myself creating complex masks containing multiple layers projected upon each other. I decided to produce this composition as an engraving on its own. My ambition was to engrave it upon an original piece of the Ilen. My thoughts where that these plaques could be presented to patrons of the charity as an expression of gratitude.
