Project Description

The “free bird” started life as a pencil sketch I composed 5 years ago. In its rendition I explored the concept of a grand architect, and tried to imagine how he might design all the wonderful creatures that surround the world around us. Ive always found anatomy pictures captivating. So I wanted to reflect that depictive style whilst retaining creative freedom. The depiction of its robotic bone structure afforded me this sense of creative freedom in an otherwise regimented photo realistic sketch.
